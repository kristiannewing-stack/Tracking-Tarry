
# Code of Conduct  
  
## Our Pledge  
In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in the **Windows 11 System Monitoring Tool (Tracking Tarry)** project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.  
  
## Our Standards  
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:  
  
- Using welcoming and inclusive language  
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences  
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism  
- Focusing on what is best for the community  
- Showing empathy towards other community members  
  
Unacceptable behavior includes:  
  
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form  
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, or personal attacks  
- Public or private harassment  
- Publishing others’ private information without consent  
- Any other conduct deemed inappropriate by project maintainers that is not listed here
  
## Code Review Policy  
To maintain high code quality and project integrity:  
  
- **All code must be reviewed by at least one other team member before merging.**  
- Pull requests should include clear descriptions of changes and reasoning.  
- Reviewers are expected to provide constructive feedback and ensure adherence to project standards.  
  
## Enforcement  
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and acted upon promptly and fairly.
